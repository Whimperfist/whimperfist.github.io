About Jack(is a girl)

Post Punk Conceptualist

a miner of the sonic landscape I am equally inspired by jackhammers and sirens as I am traditional instruments.

Through the years I have collaborated with noise collectives, multimedia artists, chamber groups, artpunks and wordsmiths.

Sounds excite and sounds lull.

It spurs ponderance of the nature of its force

for Noise can be quietening. Silence can be deafening.

I can only conclude that if Noise is rhythm then Silence is rhythm too.
