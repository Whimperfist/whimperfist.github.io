Sights and Sounds

Offerings(excerpt) was recorded live at CBGB's way back in 1996. At that time I was playing with ace musicians Mark, Mary and Dave. A strange ditty that was inspired by a good friend who believed in being unapologetically oneself.

Safe(excerpt) is another piece from the same CBGB's show. Same motley crew of musicians. At that time I kept thinking how the quest for safety can become maniacal. Obsessions can be fueled and guided that in our fervor for safety we ignore the true danger.

My Throat is Dry (excerpt) is from 2018. Part of the larger composition The Harry Partch Suite. What can I say? The man is legend. Who wouldn't be inspired by him? We should all go freight hopping. If you do send me a letter.
